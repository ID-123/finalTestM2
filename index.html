About us

Falling in Reverse, founded in 2008 by Ronnie Radke, blends post-hardcore with rap and electronic elements, largely formed while Radke was incarcerated. The band, initially named "From Behind These Walls," has achieved over 6.3 billion streams, with hit "Popular Monster" reaching No. 1 on Billboard’s Mainstream Rock chart.
